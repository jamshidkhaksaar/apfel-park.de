# Initial Concept
The objective is to develop "Fixtech," a premium WordPress e-commerce and service website for a mobile and technology shop based in Germany. The site will cater to a German-speaking audience, offering sales of smartphones, game consoles, laptops, and accessories, alongside professional device repair services. The design will maintain the established "Dark Luxury" aesthetic.

# Product Guide: Fixtech

## Vision
To create a sophisticated, high-trust digital storefront for a premier German technology retailer. The platform will seamlessly blend a luxury e-commerce experience for high-end devices and accessories with a streamlined, professional booking interface for repair services, all presented in a "Dark Luxury" design.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** German consumers seeking high-quality smartphones, laptops, gaming consoles, and premium accessories.
- **Secondary:** Individuals and businesses requiring reliable, professional repair services for their mobile and tech devices.
- **Language:** German (Primary).

## Core Features
- **E-Commerce (WooCommerce):** Comprehensive catalog for smartphones, game consoles, laptops, and mobile accessories (cases, chargers).
- **Service Booking System:** Functionality for customers to inquire about or book repair services for their devices.
- **Dark Luxury Design System:** A high-end visual identity using `apfel-black` and `apfel-gold` to convey quality and exclusivity.
- **Localization:** Full German language support across the frontend and customer notifications.
- **Responsive & Performance:** Optimized for mobile shopping and fast load times.

## Design Principles
- **Premium Retail Showcase:** Product imagery and layout should rival high-end fashion or luxury car presentations.
- **Trust & Clarity:** Clear pricing, detailed service descriptions, and transparent repair processes to build customer confidence.
- **Immersive Brand Experience:** Leveraging the dark and gold palette to create a distinctive, memorable shopping environment.
